Who We Are

Protectors of Prosperity

GUARD FINANCE LTDA is a dedicated financial intermediary based in Fortaleza, Ceara. Led by Jessica Franca De Queiroz, we operate with a singular mission: to safeguard and grow the assets of our clients through strategic credit and insurance solutions.

We specialize in "Activities Related to Credit Intermediation," bridging the gap between individuals, businesses, and major banking institutions. However, we go further by integrating "Insurance Related Activities," ensuring that every loan or investment is backed by a safety net.

Whether you need access to capital for expansion or protection against unforeseen risks, Guard Finance stands as a vigilant partner, ensuring your financial foundation remains unshakable.
