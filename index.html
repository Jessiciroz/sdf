Our Profile

Institutional Reliability

AW FINANCE CORRESPONDENTE BANCARIO LTDA is a specialized financial intermediary located in Rio de Janeiro. Led by Augusto Parente Goncalves Magalhaes, we operate as a dedicated "Banking Correspondent," facilitating essential financial services on behalf of major banks.

We understand that navigating the credit landscape can be daunting. Our role is to simplify the process. We leverage our institutional partnerships to provide you with direct access to credit lines, deposit banking functions, and tailored financial products.

Whether you are seeking personal credit or business financing, AW Finance offers the security of a bank with the personalized attention of a dedicated consultant.
